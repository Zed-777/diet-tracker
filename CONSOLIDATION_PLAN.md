### B. MEAL CONSOLIDATION RULES

**Rule 1: Rice-based bowls**
- All stir-fries use brown rice (not noodles)
- All Asian dishes use brown rice + soy sauce + sesame oil (standardize)
- Noodle recipes eliminated entirely

**Rule 2: Grain rotation (only 2 bases)**
- Brown rice (Asian, Mediterranean, grain bowls)
- Rolled oats (breakfast base)
- No pasta, couscous, quinoa, noodles, bagels, rice cakes

**Rule 3: Protein rotation (6 anchors)**
- Chicken breast (lunch/dinner primary)
- Turkey mince (variation on chicken)
- Eggs (breakfast anchor)
- Salmon (weekly fish option)
- Greek yogurt (breakfast/snack anchor)
- Protein powder (breakfast/snack anchor)

**Rule 4: Vegetable combinations**
- Every lunch/dinner includes cherry tomatoes OR broccoli
- Use onion/garlic in every stir-fry
- Spinach appears in 3 breakfast, 2 lunch/dinner options
- Bell peppers in 2 stir-fries, rest uses broccoli

**Rule 5: Sauce standardization**
- Soy + sesame oil (Asian)
- Olive oil + lemon (Mediterranean)
- Passata (Italian)
- Greek yogurt (creamy/dressing base)
- Eliminate: oyster sauce, pad thai sauce, caesar dressing, curry paste

## PHASE 4: NUTRITIONAL VALIDATION REQUIREMENTS

### Ensure Coverage:
✓ **Lose Weight Goal** (5 recipes minimum)
- Low fat, high protein
- Example: Grilled chicken + broccoli + brown rice (low cal, high fiber)

✓ **Build Muscle Goal** (5 recipes minimum)
- High protein, moderate-high calorie
- Example: Breakfast burrito with eggs + turkey + whole wheat bread

✓ **Definition Goal** (5 recipes minimum)
- High protein, low carb
- Example: Cottage cheese + berries + granola

✓ **Maintenance Goal** (5 recipes minimum)
- Balanced macros
- Example: Chicken + sweet potato + mixed greens

### Macro Targets:
- **Breakfast:** 350-550 cal; 20-40g protein; 40-60g carbs; 8-20g fat
- **Lunch:** 400-550 cal; 40-60g protein; 40-60g carbs; 8-20g fat
- **Dinner:** 500-600 cal; 45-60g protein; 45-60g carbs; 15-25g fat
- **Snacks:** 150-300 cal; 8-25g protein; 15-35g carbs; 3-15g fat
